Drawscape

Drawscape is a dynamic drawing game where your face becomes the canvas. Each prompt challenges you to draw different designs or objects directly on your own face using the camera display. Freeze the frame to perfect your masterpiece and save it as a PNG image. Unleash your creativity, have fun, and create memorable face artworks with Drawscape!

SerpentSway

SerpentSway is an immersive snake game that puts you in control of a slithering serpent using just your finger. Navigate through the virtual world, collecting food to grow your snake while avoiding collisions with the boundaries and your own body. The intuitive finger control adds an interactive twist, allowing you to guide the snake's movements with precision and agility. Challenge yourself to achieve the highest score and become the ultimate SerpentSway master!
